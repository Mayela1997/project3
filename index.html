San Salvador, El Salvador

San Salvador is the Capital of the smallest country in Central America and the official language is Spanish.The Metropolitan Area of San Salvador has 19 municipalities, and it has a population of 2,940,053. El Salvador is a beautiful country that many people should visit, in this 2024 is one of the safest countries in Latin America. There are Volcanos with beautiful views, the closest beach is at 40 minutes from the capital, depending of traffic. A lot of tourist love going to El Salvador for the culture, the food, the nature, the beaches and the people is very friendly. I will leave 4 turistic ideas in case you are looking for something to do in the Capital, you definely need to visit this places and you won't regret it.

Metropolitan Cathedral of San Salvador

The cathedral site is the place where the old Temple of Santo Domingo (dedicated to St. Dominic) once stood.The cathedral was established in 1842 and destroyed in an 1873 earthquake.The second wooden cathedral, completed in 1999.The church was twice visited by Pope John Paul II who said that the cathedral was "intimately allied with the joys and hopes of the Salvadoran people.The catedral is very impresive from the inside with a lot of paintings and stained glass windows. Monument to the Divine Savior of the World

The monument was built in November 26, 1942 and the height is 59 ft. This monument it is a symbol that identifies and represents both El Salvador and Salvadorans throughout the world. The main religious celebration is held on August 6th, as a procession is held were the monument is, every year thousands of citizens and turists come to the celebration, there is a fair for a week and this has with mechanical games, food and small business.
